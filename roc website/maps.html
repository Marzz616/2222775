Docks

Docks in Call of Duty Mobile features an intricate layout perfect for tactical gameplay. With its narrow walkways and close-quarters combat zones, it favors quick reflexes and strategic maneuvers. The map's verticality and multiple access points provide opportunities for ambushes and flanking, catering to diverse playstyles. Its gritty, industrial setting adds to the immersive experience, with detailed textures and ambient sounds enhancing the realism. Docks is ideal for intense, fast-paced matches, offering a balanced environment that keeps players on their toes.


Shoot House

Shoot House in Call of Duty Mobile is a compact, action-packed map designed for fast-paced combat. Its straightforward layout with a mix of open areas and tight corridors promotes constant engagements and rapid movement. The central lane offers long sightlines perfect for marksmen, while the side routes cater to aggressive, close-quarters combat. The training facility setting with its realistic military aesthetics adds to the immersive experience. Shoot House is ideal for players who enjoy intense, high-octane matches, providing a balanced arena where quick decision-making and reflexes are key to victory.
